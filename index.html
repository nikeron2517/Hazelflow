The

Blockchain teams (especially high-tech ones) struggle to clearly and meaningfully explain their complex product, vision, and the value proposition behind it.

So, what’s the way to fix this?

We are a team of researchers and writers with over 5 years of experience in research , analytics, and the creation of technical, product, and educational materials.

We research and identify the core difficulties of a protocol and propose directions for addressing them.

Based on this, we craft a strategy for technical, product, and educational content.

This content helps teams to effectively articulate their value proposition, technical architecture, and product features to a broader audience.

When we say "broader audience" we refer to three main categories of users

High-profile individuals

Developers, researchers, investors, and analysts deeply involved in the blockchain space.

Degens

Community members, contributors, traders, and other active ecosystem users.

Newcomers

Those who have recently entered the crypto space or the protocol's ecosystem in general.

We are developing a strategic content plan to ensure that all user categories clearly understand the protocol’s purpose and value. Our primary goal: when people think of a specific blockchain vertical, our client’s name is the first that comes to mind.

Frequently
Questions

Is the research and article writing done entirely by humans?

Yes, our research and article writing are conducted entirely by humans. However, we use AI to correct spelling errors and grammar.

Which types of protocols do you work with?

We primarily work with infrastructure projects, privacy protocols, crypto-AI teams, DeFi, and consumer crypto. We are always open to discussing other blockchain verticals.

How are engagements usually structured?

We specialize in long-term partnerships lasting 6 months or more, focusing on deep engagement and clear communication. However, we are open to discussing alternative ways of collaborating.

What are the benefits of a long-term partnership with you?

Because our collaborations extend over long periods, our team gains a thorough understanding of the product. This deep engagement allows us to conduct deep research and produce insightful and informative articles. We deliver high-quality content by fully engaging with the protocol over time.

How do you keep the client informed about the progress of the work? How often do you provide updates?

We ensure complete transparency in our interactions with clients at every stage of the process, including communication and progress tracking. To achieve this, we create a table in Notion where we update task statuses in real time and add the client’s team members. You can access the table anytime to check the current status of the project. To ensure open communication, we create a chat where our team stays in constant contact with the client, promptly updates on the progress, and answers all your questions.

Which services are included in your technical content creation?

We offer a wide range of services: we write technical articles tailored to different target audiences, create content for your media channels, and prepare whitepapers and other technical documents.

Do you create diagrams and visual illustrations for your content?

Yes, we create diagrams from scratch or enhance existing ones. For instance, we collaborated with Sonic Labs (formerly Fantom Foundation) and produced numerous diagrams for their whitepaper.

Do you offer any other services?

Besides our core offering, we also create technical docs, whitepapers, and work with teams on private research, which is utilized internally and not published anywhere. If you want to leverage our time for any other research- and writing-related activity, we are keen to discuss.

How much time does it usually take to write content?

Highly technical articles requiring in-depth research, such as those involving extensive analysis or primary source material, will demand more time. Conversely, articles based on pre-existing ideas or insights can typically be completed and refined within one week. For instance, a comprehensive report on the broader landscape, if approved, might require reviewing multiple whitepapers, analyzing on-chain data, and consuming hours of panels and podcasts. Such an undertaking could require an entire month or even more for a single article.
